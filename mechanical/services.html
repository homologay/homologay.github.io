Services

Mobile mechanic services:
I fix: bicycles, ebikes, escooters, and mobility devices (wheelchairs, walkers, canes, electric mobility scooters). I fix most bicycles, but very high-end ones are not my focus as they often have incompatible parts and need special tools.
-Fee to show up: $20 for Vancouver, $30 for UBC/Burnaby/Richmond/New West, and $45 for North Van, West Van, Coquitlam, PoMo, PoCo, Surrey, Langley, and Delta.
-Hourly rate: $40/hr time measured by rounding to nearest half hour.

-Hourly packages: Basic tune / safety check (1hr), basic tune and deep clean (2hr), Advanced tune: tune, deep clean, and bearing service (3.5hr) .

Educational services: Workshops: $100 for 1hr, $150 for 2hr. I can offer 1-on-1 education at my usual mechanic rates. Let me know what your educational needs and we can work something out.
